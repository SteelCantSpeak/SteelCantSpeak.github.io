About Me

As an individual with autism, I bring a unique and creative perspective to my work, often channeling my passion into developing small-scale games or websites with quirky gimmicks like 3D rendering or animation (though, admittedly, the graphics might not always be top-notch). My deep focus and attention to detail fuel my projects, allowing me to explore innovative ideas and unconventional approaches. Outside of my professional work, I find joy in playing casual games such as Animal Crossing and Minecraft, which provide a relaxing and fun balance to my tech-driven endeavors. This blend of creativity, meticulousness, and playfulness defines who I am and shapes the projects I undertake.
